I'm an adventurer

My next greatest passion is adventure. This often manifests itself in traveling, but even when I'm not traveling I seek adventure in my every day.

When I was 17, I lived in Okinawa, Japan for 8 months, where I studied at Calvary Chapel Bible College. I then stayed 2 months in Daejeon, South Korea.

Most recently, my wife and I embarked on a 6-month backpacking trip through Southeast Asia. We spent a month each in Thailand, Cambodia, Vietnam, Philippines, Indonesia, and Malaysia. On this trip, we went on 24 scuba dives, climbed a volcano, swam in waterfalls, played with elephants and tigers, drove motorbikes through the pouring rain, saw a tree full of fireflies, visited the ancient city of Angkor Wat, and so much more.

When I'm not traveling, my adventursome nature is satisfied by learning. I constantly seek knowledge and skill. If I'm not working or spending time with my wife, I'm learning. And saving money for the next trip. ;)
